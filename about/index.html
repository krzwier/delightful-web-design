Hi there, my name is Karen Zwier! I have an eclectic background and I’m also a go-getter, which means I will tackle just about anything. I thrive on bringing order out of disorder, whether it be in intellectual writing, streamlining organizational processes and procedures, or the building of physical objects out of parts and pieces. Here are some of the many things I do:

  • I am branching out into web and software development (leaving an academic career behind—see below). Technical tools in my toolkit include Java, SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Git. And because of my career as an educator, I'm particularly interested in educational technology.
  • I am a "recovering" academic. I earned a Ph.D. in History and Philosophy of Science from the University of Pittsburgh. I have taught at a few universities and lectured on a wide range of topics that fall at the intersection of science, philosophy, and religion. If you're interested in that other life, my CV can be found here.
  • I am a mother of 4 awesome kids.
  • I love building and fixing things. I’m an avid DIY’er who fixes garbage disposals, builds bunk beds, repairs patio doors, and hangs ceiling fans. (Ok, confession time...it's not all success stories. My old gas dryer and my aging water heater both ended up in a junkyard somewhere, but not until I had stripped them down and tried every last thing!)
  • I volunteer with the PTO at my children’s school. I’m all for supporting teachers in the invaluable (and difficult) work they do!
  • I keep chickens in my backyard. Yes, I built the coop myself, and I ended up designing a custom watering device that has withstood the occasional -20°F temperatures of Iowa winters.

Ok, that's enough bragging. All you really need to know is that I'll work hard for you and deliver a high-quality product, so hire me!
